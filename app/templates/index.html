Welcome to Farm Aid


We at Farm Aid help farmers who face dilemmas while nurturing their crops and help them pave their way to the appropriate buyers who can provide the best cost for their harvest. This website is a one stop solution for your queries related to crop prediction, harvest, news, selling and buying.

From this page you can navigate to our crop recommendation page where our website can predict the best suited crop for you, the news page which contains all recent updates about agriculture and the buyer's/farmer's connect page for getting in connection with the buyer/seller of your choice. For further queries please feel free to reach to our "Farm bot" present at the right corner of this page. You may also contact us through our mails given at the bottom of the page. We hope you have a good experience with us.

Happy Farming!
